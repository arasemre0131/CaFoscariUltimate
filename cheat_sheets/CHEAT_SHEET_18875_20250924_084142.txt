**EXAM CHEAT SHEET - COMPUTER ARCHITECTURES FOUNDATIONS**

**1. FORMULA QUICK REFERENCE**

| Concept | Formula |
|---------|---------|
| Binary to Decimal | $\sum_{i=0}^{n} b_i \cdot 2^i$ |
| Decimal to Binary | Repeated division by 2 |
| 2's Complement | $-b_n \cdot 2^n + \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} b_i \cdot 2^i$ |
| Gate Delay | $t_d = R \cdot C$ |
| Critical Path Delay | $t_{crit} = \sum_i t_{d,i}$ |
| Throughput | $\frac{1}{t_{crit}}$ |
| CPI | $\frac{\text{Total Cycles}}{\text{Total Instructions}}$ |
| MIPS | $\frac{\text{Total Instructions}}{\text{Total Cycles} \cdot \text{Clock Period}}$ |

**2. CALCULATION SHORTCUTS & TRICKS**

- Binary-Decimal conversion: Group bits, multiply and add
- Mental 2's complement: Flip bits, add 1
- Gate delay: Identify dominant R or C, estimate $t_d$
- Critical path: Trace longest delay chain, sum delays
- CPI and MIPS: Focus on cache misses, branch prediction

**3. EXAM TACTICS**

- Time management: Allocate time based on marks, tackle easy questions first
- Question approach: Understand requirements, identify key concepts, show work
- Partial credit: Clearly indicate steps, justify assumptions, provide explanations
- Error checking: Verify units, review signs, double-check calculations

**4. COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID**

- Forgetting sign bit in 2's complement
- Confusing gate delay and critical path
- Neglecting cache effects in CPI and MIPS
- Mixing up time and frequency in performance metrics
