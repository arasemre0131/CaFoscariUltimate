## Important Facts
- A set of vectors is linearly independent if none of the vectors can be expressed as a linear combination of the others.
- The dimension of a vector space is the number of vectors in any basis.
- The rank of a matrix is the dimension of the column space (or row space).
- The nullity of a matrix is the dimension of the null space.
- A matrix is diagonalizable if it has a complete set of linearly independent eigenvectors.
